Description:

Help save the city from a malicious monster unleashing chaos! Use your basic math skills to aim rockets at the creature. Only you can stop it, save the city!

How To Play:

At the start, you'll be greeted with a screen asking for the highest number you'd like to use in the equations. Once you enter a number and click Play, an equation will be generated. Solve the equation within 10 seconds to launch a rocket at the monster. If you succeed, you strike the enemy! But if you fail the monster launches a rocket at you!
